Calculator Instructions

How to use:

  • Click numbers to enter them
  • Click +, -, ×, or ÷ for operations
  • Click = to calculate
  • Click C to clear
  • Click ⌫ to delete last number

Computer Pop-up Notifications:

1. Click "Enable Computer Notifications" button

2. Allow notifications in the browser pop-up

3. When you subtract numbers, a computer notification will pop up showing the difference

Example: 10 - 3 = 7 will show a computer pop-up saying "The difference is 7"

Note: Works in Chrome, Firefox, and Edge browsers
